\subsection{Detailed Description}
The Epanechnikov kernel, defined as. 

\[ K(x, y) = \max \{0, 1 - || x - y ||^2_2 / b^2 \} \]

where $ b $ is the bandwidth the of the kernel (defaults to 1.\+0).
